ACME CORPORATION - EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
New Employee Handbook | HR Department | 2024
=============================================

1. FIRST DAY CHECKLIST
------------------------
✓ Report to Reception at 9:00 AM on your first day
✓ Bring original ID documents for verification
✓ Meet your assigned HR buddy at Reception
✓ Collect your ID badge and access card
✓ Receive company laptop and mobile device from IT
✓ Sign and submit all onboarding documents
✓ Complete emergency contact form

2. WEEK 1 SCHEDULE
--------------------
Day 1: HR Orientation — Company overview, policies, benefits
Day 2: IT Setup — Systems access, email, tools training
Day 3: Department Introduction — Meet your team and manager
Day 4: Role-specific training begins
Day 5: Review and Q&A session with HR

3. SYSTEMS ACCESS SETUP
-------------------------
Email: Your corporate email is firstname.lastname@acmecorp.com
HR Portal: hr.acmecorp.com (leave, payroll, benefits)
IT Portal: helpdesk.acmecorp.com (support tickets, software)
Learning Portal: learn.acmecorp.com (training courses)
Project Management: projects.acmecorp.com (Jira, Confluence)
Communication: Microsoft Teams (installed on your laptop)

4. PROBATION PERIOD
--------------------
All new employees undergo a 6-month probation period.
Performance reviews are conducted at 1 month, 3 months, and 6 months.
Manager feedback is provided at each review milestone.
Successful completion leads to permanent employment confirmation.
During probation: limited leave, no bonus eligibility, no internal transfer.

5. EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
---------------------
Health Insurance: Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
Life Insurance: Coverage equal to 2x annual salary
Retirement Plan: 401(k) with 5% company match
Annual Bonus: Performance-based, paid in January
Learning Budget: $2,000 per year for courses and certifications
Gym Membership: 50% subsidy for gym memberships
Employee Assistance Program: Free counseling and mental health support

6. CODE OF CONDUCT
-------------------
Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism.
Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately.
Confidentiality of company information must be maintained at all times.
Social media posts must not disclose confidential company information.
Dress code is business casual Monday to Thursday, casual on Fridays.

7. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
---------------------------
Annual performance reviews are conducted every December.
Goals are set at the beginning of each year using SMART framework.
Mid-year check-in is conducted in June.
Performance ratings: Outstanding, Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement.
Promotion decisions are based on performance ratings and business needs.

8. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT
-----------------------------
ACME encourages continuous learning and professional development.
All employees must complete mandatory compliance training annually.
Learning Portal offers 500+ online courses.
External certifications are reimbursed up to $2,000 per year.
Mentorship program is available — apply via HR Portal.
Internal job postings are available after 1 year of service.

9. PAYROLL INFORMATION
-----------------------
Salary is paid on the last working day of each month.
Payslips are available on the HR Portal.
Direct deposit is the standard payment method.
Overtime is paid at 1.5x the hourly rate for non-exempt employees.
Expense reimbursements are processed within 5 business days.
Tax documents (W-2) are available by January 31 each year.
