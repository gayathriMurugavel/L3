ACME CORPORATION - HUMAN RESOURCES LEAVE POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
================================================

1. ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY
----------------------
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis.
Leave must be approved by the direct manager at least 5 working days in advance.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days to the next calendar year.
Leave encashment is allowed for a maximum of 5 days per year.

2. SICK LEAVE POLICY
--------------------
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per calendar year.
A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next calendar year.
Employees on probation are entitled to 6 days of sick leave only.

3. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
---------------------------------
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave.
Maternity leave can commence 8 weeks before the expected delivery date.
Adoption leave of 12 weeks is available for primary caregivers.

4. EMERGENCY AND BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
------------------------------------
Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid emergency leave per year.
Bereavement leave of 5 days is granted for immediate family members.
Immediate family includes spouse, children, parents, and siblings.
Extended bereavement of 2 days is available for grandparents and in-laws.

5. LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
------------------------------
Step 1: Log in to the HR Portal at hr.acmecorp.com
Step 2: Navigate to Leave Management section
Step 3: Select leave type and enter dates
Step 4: Add remarks and submit for approval
Step 5: Manager receives email notification
Step 6: Employee receives approval or rejection via email

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
------------------
ACME Corporation observes all 14 national public holidays.
If a public holiday falls on a weekend, a compensatory holiday is granted.
Employees required to work on public holidays receive double pay.

7. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
---------------------------
Employees may apply for leave without pay for personal reasons.
LWP must be approved by both manager and HR department.
Maximum LWP allowed is 30 days per calendar year.
LWP impacts annual increment calculations proportionally.

8. NEW EMPLOYEE LEAVE ENTITLEMENT
-----------------------------------
Employees in probation period (first 6 months) earn leave on pro-rata basis.
Leave cannot be availed during probation without special approval from HR.
Full leave entitlement begins after successful completion of probation.
