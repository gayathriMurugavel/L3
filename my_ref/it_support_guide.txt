ACME CORPORATION - IT SUPPORT AND HELPDESK GUIDE
Version 3.0 | IT Department | Updated: March 2024
==================================================

1. HOW TO SUBMIT A SUPPORT TICKET
-----------------------------------
Step 1: Visit the IT Helpdesk Portal at helpdesk.acmecorp.com
Step 2: Click on "New Ticket" button on the dashboard
Step 3: Select the appropriate category:
        - Hardware Issues
        - Software Issues
        - Network and Connectivity
        - Email and Communication
        - Access and Permissions
        - Security Incidents
Step 4: Fill in the subject line with a clear description
Step 5: Describe the issue in detail including error messages
Step 6: Attach screenshots if applicable
Step 7: Set priority level: Low, Medium, High, or Critical
Step 8: Click Submit — you will receive a ticket number via email

2. TICKET PRIORITY LEVELS
--------------------------
CRITICAL: System outage affecting multiple users — Response: 1 hour
HIGH:     Single user cannot work at all — Response: 4 hours
MEDIUM:   Work impacted but workaround available — Response: 8 hours
LOW:      Minor issue or general question — Response: 24 hours

3. SOFTWARE INSTALLATION POLICY
---------------------------------
All software installations must be approved by IT department.
Employees must NOT install unauthorized software on company devices.
Submit a software request via the IT portal with business justification.
Approved software list is maintained at: it.acmecorp.com/approved-software
IT department will install approved software within 2 business days.

4. PASSWORD POLICY
-------------------
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
Must contain uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
Previous 10 passwords cannot be reused.
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all accounts.
Never share your password with anyone including IT staff.

5. VPN AND REMOTE ACCESS
--------------------------
Download the VPN client from: it.acmecorp.com/vpn
Use your corporate credentials to connect.
VPN must be active when accessing company resources from outside office.
VPN auto-disconnects after 8 hours of inactivity.
Report any VPN issues to helpdesk@acmecorp.com

6. EMAIL GUIDELINES
--------------------
Corporate email must be used for all official communications.
Maximum email attachment size is 25 MB.
Use OneDrive or SharePoint links for large files.
Do not use corporate email for personal communications.
Phishing emails must be reported to: security@acmecorp.com

7. HARDWARE REQUEST PROCESS
-----------------------------
Submit hardware requests via IT Portal under "Asset Request"
New hire hardware is provisioned within 3 business days.
Hardware upgrades require manager approval and budget allocation.
Damaged hardware must be reported to IT immediately.
Lost or stolen devices must be reported within 1 hour to security@acmecorp.com

8. COMMON TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS
---------------------------------
Slow Computer:
- Restart the computer
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Check for pending Windows updates
- Contact IT if issue persists

Cannot Connect to Internet:
- Check network cable or WiFi connection
- Restart router if working from home
- Disable and re-enable network adapter
- Submit ticket if unresolved

Forgot Password:
- Visit: password.acmecorp.com
- Click "Reset Password"
- Enter your employee email
- Follow instructions sent to your recovery email
